    What I'm Listening To Right Now

    Process, Sampha

    You may have never heard of the name, but you'll definitely recognize the voice. London-based singer-songwriter Sampha has made quite the name for himself in the music industry, featuring on tracks with Drake, Frank Ocean, Kanye West, and Solange with his emotive, breathy, gorgeously otherworldly voice.

    But frankly, Process blows all of this illustrious previous work out of the water. A haunting album about grief and loss with heavy influences from James Blake and American R&B, Process is a emotional, devastating listen from start to finish, and my top album of 2017 so far.

  • We're New Here, Jamie xx

    An under-the-radar release featuring musical legend Gil Scott-Heron, this album exudes energy, warmth, and color.

  • Steroids Megamix, Death Grips

    Death Grips' aggressive and nihilistic style isn't for everyone, but if you can stomach it it's certainly worth a listen.

  • To Pimp a Butterfly, Kendrick Lamar

    With Kendrick's new project out and this album, you're reminded of just how exceptional of an artist Kendrick really is.
